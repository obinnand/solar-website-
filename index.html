Partial Installation Services

Solar-only installation

If you already possess an inverter, consider enhancing its capabilities by incorporating solar panels to supplement its charging from the PHCN grid. Discover the cost of integrating solar into your current inverter setup instantly through our solar-only cost estimator.

Inverter-only installation

If your PHCN power supply is reliable, opting for an inverter alone can ensure a continuous power source during PHCN outages. This approach allows you to initiate your solar power journey in a budget-friendly manner without committing to the complete solar investment upfront. Additionally, you have the flexibility to incorporate solar panels later on when it aligns with your timing and preferences.

Full Solar Installation

Experience nationwide solar system and inverter installations encompassing solar panels, inverters, batteries, and more. Our installations include the isolation of inverter and non-inverter circuits, allowing you to power essential appliances through your solar system and heavier appliances via PHCN or a generator. Additionally, we provide thorough earthing solutions to safeguard your solar equipment against lightning strikes.
